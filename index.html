About Me

As a 2026 Master of Architecture graduate from Pratt Institute, my design philosophy is rooted in the synergy between advanced technology and environmental longevity. My academic and research trajectory has been dedicated to exploring how Digital Twins and data-driven optimization can enhance urban resilience and architectural performance. Throughout my tenure at Pratt and prior training, I have developed a robust technical toolkit in BIM, Revit, Rhino/Grasshopper, and 3D fabrication, complemented by a keen eye for schematic clarity and construction documentation. I am a collaborative problem-solver who thrives at the intersection of complex systems and human-centric design. I am actively looking for Full-time Architectural Designer opportunities to apply my skills in a professional setting and contribute to shaping a resilient built environment.
